Sanghyun Han

Electrical and Computer Engineering, Seoul National University

CV Email Blog

Sanghyun Han is a Ph.D student in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at Seoul National University. He received B.S. degree from Yonsei University in 2020.

His research ultimately aims at expanding computing power of mobile devices through networking. The research comprised of several sub-topics such as 5G/6G, virtualization, machine learning, etc. For more detailed information on the research topics mentioned above, please refer to his Blog .

He is currently affiliated with Networked Computing Lab (NXC) led by Prof. Kyunghan Lee.
